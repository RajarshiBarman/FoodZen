About Us

There was a time when anyone craved for their favorite street food, they had to go out, browse a number of stalls, ask and negotiate prices with more number of such street food vendors. Then after a long hasty work, finally find and indulge in their food in peace. But let's be real. Does anyone want to work more to eat their favorite food, after having a long hasty day at work. Not really, especially not in cities booming with business. But not now. Now, with FoodZen, you can order your favorite street food and choose from a wide variety of options. Yes! we provide doorstep delivery so you do not need to go out of your home. Just open your door please, when we knock!
